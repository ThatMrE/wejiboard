What is Weji?

Ouija for the
Emoji Era

Weji is a laser-cut wooden spirit board featuring a hand-selected grid of emoji instead of the traditional alphabet. The planchette — hand-crafted from dark walnut — slides across the board to point at emoji, letting the spirits communicate in the only language that truly transcends the mortal plane.

Is the ghost happy? Sad? Did it want pizza? Now you’ll know. Weji was created at Global Game Jam and has been haunting living rooms, antique shops, and curiosity cabinets ever since.

The spirits have always had opinions. Now they have options.

The Ritual

How to Summon

I

Gather

Assemble 2–4 seekers. Dim the lights. Place the Weji board on a flat surface. Light a candle if the mood strikes.

II

Place

All players lightly rest fingertips on the walnut planchette. Do not grip. The spirits guide — you merely accompany.

III

Ask

One player speaks a question aloud. Open-ended works best. The spirits have feelings. Don’t ask boring questions.

IV

Receive

The planchette moves to the answer. Emoji. Plural if necessary. Interpret accordingly. Debate enthusiastically.
